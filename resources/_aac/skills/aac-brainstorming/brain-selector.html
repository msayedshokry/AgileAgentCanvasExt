Brainstorming Technique Selector

A self-contained picker for the brainstorming technique catalog. Filter by goal, copy any technique to your clipboard. Press Ctrl+K to focus the search.

Strategy: pick a category by your goal

  • Stuck on assumptions? Try creative (What If, Reversal, First Principles, Forced Relationships, Cross-Pollination).
  • Need broader perspective? Try collaborative (Role Playing, Build on What Works, How Might We) or cultural.
  • Deep root cause? Try deep (Five Whys, Fishbone, JTBD, Empathy Map, TRIZ).
  • Planning forward? Try strategic (Backcasting, Scenario Cross) or structured.
  • User-centred framing? Try deep (Empathy Map, JTBD).
  • Permission to play? Try wild, theatrical, or introspective_delight.
