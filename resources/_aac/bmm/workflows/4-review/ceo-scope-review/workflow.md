# CEO Scope Review Workflow

This workflow challenges a product spec (Product Brief or PRD) to calibrate its ambition level.

## 1. Scope Calibration
Analyze the current scope of the artifact. You must select one of the four modes to frame your review:
- **Scope Expansion**: The plan is too timid. What would a "10-star" version of this product look like? Make it massive.
- **Selective Expansion**: The core is right, but a specific wedge or feature needs to be significantly bolder.
- **Hold Scope**: The ambition is perfectly tuned to the resources and market timing. Defend this boundaries.
- **Scope Reduction**: The plan is bloated. Strip it down to the bare minimum "duct-tape" MVP. What is the absolute minimum required to prove value?

## 2. The Challenge
Based on your selected mode, write out a direct, "executive-level" challenge to the user. Elicit their reaction to your proposed scope shift. Use the "Six Forcing Questions" if relevant (Why now? Who has their hair on fire?).

## 3. Recommend Amendments
Draft specific amendments to the artifact to align it with the calibrated scope. Ask the user for permission to apply these changes or write them to a `ceo-review.md` artifact.
